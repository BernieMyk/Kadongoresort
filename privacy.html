1. Information We Collect

At Kadongo Resort, we collect information to provide you with an exceptional stay experience. This includes:

  • Personal Information: Name, contact details, passport/ID for registration, payment information (processed securely via PCI-compliant providers).
  • Stay Preferences: Room type, dietary requirements, special requests, arrival/departure dates.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information when you visit our website (via cookies).
  • Communication Records: Emails, chat logs, or call notes when you contact our guest services.

We do not collect sensitive personal data (racial origin, health data, etc.) unless voluntarily provided for specific service needs (e.g., medical assistance requests).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information enables us to:

  • Process reservations, payments, and check-in/check-out procedures
  • Personalize your stay experience and anticipate your preferences
  • Communicate booking confirmations, pre-arrival details, and post-stay follow-ups
  • Improve our services, website functionality, and guest offerings
  • Comply with legal obligations (Kenyan tourism regulations, tax requirements)
  • Protect the security of our property, guests, and staff

We never sell your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who assist with bookings, payments, or communications (bound by confidentiality agreements).
  • Legal Requirements: When required by Kenyan law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (guests will be notified).
  • With Your Consent: For specific purposes you explicitly authorize.

4. Your Rights & Choices

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities
  • Request data portability in a structured format
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with Kenya's Office of the Data Protection Commissioner

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@kadongoresort.com. We respond within 30 days.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard safeguards:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls limiting data to authorized personnel only
  • Secure payment processing via PCI-DSS compliant providers
  • Staff training on data protection best practices

No system is 100% secure. If you suspect a breach, notify us immediately.

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and login status
  • Analyze site usage to improve functionality (via Google Analytics)
  • Enable booking engine functionality
  • Personalize content and offers

You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may limit website functionality. See our Cookie Policy for details.

7. International Data Transfers

Kadongo Resort is based in Kenya. If you are visiting from outside Kenya, your information may be transferred to and processed in Kenya, which has data protection laws that may differ from your jurisdiction. We ensure appropriate safeguards (standard contractual clauses, adequacy decisions) are in place for such transfers.
