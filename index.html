Strategically positioned near the main transport points on the island, the resort offers flexibility and convenience for travelers. Just a short walk from the local boda boda stage, allowing guests to move around the island comfortably.

Mawanga Rock Art Cave

Ancient rock paintings with concentric circles believed to be 1,000-4,000 years old. A site of cultural and spiritual significance, historically associated with rainmaking rituals.
